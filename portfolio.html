Onomatopoetic words

I created a poster based on an onomatopoeic word, "screech," aiming to illustrate both its meaning and sound. I selected a thicker font to blend with the asphalt and incorporated smoke in the background to evoke the imagery of a car drifting.

Porsche poster

Porsche poster

I aimed to create a sleek and simple car poster inspired by recent trends. Choosing the Porsche 911 for its blend of sharp and round shapes, I focused on using only the colors present on the car itself. Maintaining a minimalist approach, I utilized ample whitespace to draw attention to the car as the focal point, avoiding clutter and ensuring its prominence in the design.

Coca cola poster

Coca Cola poster

For a product photography assignment in my photo class, I decided to capture an empty Coke can due to its vibrant color. To enhance its impact, I opted for a gray background, ensuring the can stood out prominently. With limited time for the task, I quickly added the Coke logo and used the Snickers slogan in a playful manner, as this aspect of the assignment was less formal.

TikTok redesign

TikTok redesign

At the time, redesigning an app was the most significant project I had undertaken to develop my skills. I selected TikTok for this endeavor, as I had observed numerous flaws and questionable elements in its interface.

I began by analyzing various social platform apps to gather both inspiration and identify elements I wanted to avoid. Subsequently, I created a mood board, incorporating elements from these apps along with images that aligned with TikTok's aesthetic. Over the course of several weeks, I iterated on the project, refining it through continuous adjustments. By the project's conclusion, I felt satisfied with the outcome, achieving a simpler design while preserving TikTok's original style.
